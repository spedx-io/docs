# PnL

Traders can earn profit by taking positions in derivatives markets. Profits can be multiplied with the use of leverage.
The same applies to losses as well. Profits or losses from derivatives trading that are not yet realized or settled are known
as Unrealized PnL. PnL is paid in USDC.

## What is realization of PnL

Realization of PnL is an event wherein a trader closes his open derivative position at a profit / loss. However, the trader has not paid /
received the PnL yet.

## What is settlement of PnL

Settlement of PnL is the event following PnL realization wherein the realized PnL is settled against a PnL Pool. In this stage, the trader
actually pays / receives his PnL.
